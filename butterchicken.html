BUTTER CHICKEN

Description:

Butter chicken is a popular Indian dish made with tender chicken cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. It's mildly spiced and known for its buttery flavor and smooth texture.


Ingredients:

  • Chicken (boneless, usually thighs or breasts)
  • Onion
  • Garlic-Ginger Paste
  • Tomato Paste
  • Yogurt
  • Cream
  • Garam Masala
  • Kashmiri red chili powder
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Cook the chicken in a pan or grill until browned and cooked through. Set aside.

  3. Heat butter in a pan, sauté chopped onions until golden.

  4. Add pureed tomatoes, cook until oil separates from the mixture.

  5. Add spices – garam masala, chili powder, salt – and cook for a few minutes.

  6. Pour in cream and mix well to make a smooth, rich sauce.

  7. Add the cooked chicken and kasuri methi. Simmer for 5–10 minutes.

  8. Garnish with cream or butter, and serve hot with naan or rice.
