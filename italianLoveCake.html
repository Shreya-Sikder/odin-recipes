Italian Love Cake

Description:

Italian Love Cake is a delightful dessert that combines a rich chocolate cake with a creamy ricotta cheese layer, topped with a light dusting of powdered sugar. This cake is known for its unique texture and flavor, making it a favorite for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 box chocolate cake mix
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the chocolate cake mix, 2 eggs, water, and vegetable oil. Mix until well combined.
  3. Pour half of the chocolate batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  4. In another bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, remaining egg, sugar, vanilla extract, and almond extract until smooth.
  5. Spread the ricotta mixture over the chocolate batter in the pan.
  6. Pour the remaining chocolate batter over the ricotta layer, spreading it gently to cover.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cake to cool completely in the pan. Once cooled, dust the top with powdered sugar before serving.
  9. Slice the cake and enjoy!
